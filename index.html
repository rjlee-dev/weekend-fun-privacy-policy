Privacy Policy for Weekend Fun

Last updated: August 8, 2025

Weekend Fun ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Weekend Fun mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information from users. The App may store your preferences (such as budget, group size, age range, and location) locally on your device to provide tailored activity suggestions. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

2. Location Data

If you enable location services, the App uses your approximate location to suggest nearby activities. Your location data is processed only on your device and is not sent to us or stored on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying ads.
  • OpenAI – to generate activity suggestions.

These third-party services may collect anonymized usage data as outlined in their own privacy policies.

4. In-App Purchases

If you choose to subscribe or make in-app purchases, transactions are handled securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

5. Data Security

All personal preferences and data are stored locally on your device. We do not operate any external databases for user information.

6. Your Choices

You can disable location services at any time through your device settings. You can also delete the App to remove all locally stored data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

Email: support@weekendfun.app

By using Weekend Fun, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
